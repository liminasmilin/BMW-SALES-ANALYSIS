# 🚗 BMW Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Abstract

The **BMW Sales Analysis Dashboard** is a data analytics project designed to analyze BMW car sales data and generate meaningful business insights.

This project transforms raw data into an **interactive dashboard** that highlights key performance indicators (KPIs) such as revenue, sales volume, mileage distribution, and regional performance.

It helps in identifying **high-performing models, customer preferences, and market trends**, enabling better decision-making and strategic planning.

## 📖 Introduction

In the modern automobile industry, data analysis plays a crucial role in improving business performance. Companies generate large volumes of data, which must be analyzed effectively to extract useful insights.

This project focuses on analyzing BMW sales data and presenting it through a **visual dashboard** for easy understanding.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze **total sales revenue and volume**
* Study performance of different **BMW models**
* Understand **fuel type distribution**
* Compare **regional sales trends**
* Identify **customer preferences (color, engine size)**
* Build an **interactive dashboard**

---

## 📁 Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following attributes:

* **Model** – BMW car models (3 Series, 5 Series, X Series, etc.)
* **Price_USD** – Price of the car
* **Sales_Volume** – Units sold
* **Mileage_KM** – Mileage covered
* **Fuel_Type** – Diesel, Petrol, Electric, Hybrid
* **Engine_Size_L** – Engine capacity
* **Color** – Car color
* **Region** – Sales region
* **Year** – Year of sale

### 🔸 Fuel Type Analysis

* Mileage varies slightly across models
* Shows efficiency and usage differences

### 🔸 Engine Size

* Ranges between **14.4K – 15.2K CC**
* Larger engines in premium models

### 🔸 Color Preference

* Popular colors: **Black, White, Blue**
* Customer choice impacts sales

### 🔸 Regional Analysis

* 🌍 Asia leads in revenue
* North America & Middle East follow
